About me

Hello! I’m Josi Kieling, photographer, but first of all, a huge rock fan! What I seek with my photography is to expand the transcendental experience lived in a live show. Both my passions – photography and music – are intertwined, and they reach their peak when I have the possibility to be in a rock concert, feeling the energy from both the audience and the artist, while I’m also part of the equation, capturing the magic as they become one.
